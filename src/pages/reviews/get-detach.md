# Review of Get Detach

Detach is an innovative app blocker designed to help you reclaim your time by significantly reducing screen time on your iPhone. Unlike traditional methods that rely on sheer willpower, Detach introduces a unique approach by utilizing physical NFC tags to create a tangible barrier between you and the endless distractions of social media and other apps. With Detach, you can now focus on what truly matters.

## Key Features

- **NFC Tags**: Detach uses NFC technology that lets you assign specific tags to restrict access to distracting apps. This physical representation of commitment helps in building discipline.
- **Customization**: You can customize which apps to block, tailoring your focus according to your personal needs and goals.
- **Real-time Tracking**: Get insights into your usage patterns, helping you make informed decisions about your screen time moving forward.
- **User-friendly Interface**: The app is designed with simplicity in mind, making it accessible for users of all tech levels.
- **Open-source Accessibility**: For tech enthusiasts, Detach is built on open-source principles, giving you the ability to contribute and explore its code.
- **Transactional Revenue Model**: The app operates on a transactional revenue model, allowing it to generate steady income while providing value to its users.

## Pros

- **Proven Revenue Model**: Detach has a demonstrated revenue of around $500 per month, indicating its reliability and potential for growth.
- **Enhanced Productivity**: Users often report increased productivity and focus, allowing them to achieve their personal and professional goals more efficiently.
- **Innovative Approach**: The use of NFC tags offers a distinctive twist on app blocking, making it easier for individuals to detach themselves from distractions.
- **Open-source Community**: Being open-source means regular updates and opportunities for community support, fostering an engaging user environment.

## Cons

- **Limited to iOS**: Currently, Detach is only available on the iPhone, which might alienate users on other platforms.
- **Physical Investment**: The requirement for NFC tags means some upfront costs for users who do not already own these devices.
- **Simplicity Might Limit Power Users**: While the app is user-friendly, more advanced users may find it lacking in features that offer deeper control or analytics.

## Final Verdict

Overall, Detach is a compelling tool for anyone looking to reduce their screen time and increase their productivity. Its unique implementation of NFC tags sets it apart from other app blockers, and its proven revenue model suggests that it is both sustainable and valuable. Although it has some limitations, its benefits far outweigh the drawbacks for many users. If you struggle with distractions on your iPhone and wish to refocus your time effectively, Detach should certainly be on your radar.
